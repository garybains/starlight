Toddler Bed

  • Home
  • Blog Post 3

My soon to be three year old had his empty toddler bed across from his crib for almost a year. Was I scared to make the transition? You bet! I had seen what he could do to a roll of toilet paper in ten seconds. What might he do to his room in a night? Would I get sleep if he moved to his big boy bed? So many questions and worries! Why was I worried…I had finished my sleep course up to toddlers? I could nail this!!!!!

So I put what I had learned into motion. We had a chat, son, Dad and me. It was going to happen the next day for nap time! I brought it up a few more times before the next nap. Got him all ready in his sleep sack with his favourite puppy, a little story and reviewed what was going to happen. I left the room and started to watch the monitor like a hawk. Was he going to sleep? Time would tell. I suppose it is just as you or I would be trying to find our new comfy spot in a hotel bed, or a relative’s house. So did he. He wasn’t talking, he wasn’t crying, he was just settling into his new spot. And I had to let him.

It took him 40 minutes to fall asleep. I didn’t have to go to him at all. He had a bit shorter nap that afternoon as I didn’t want his sleep to go too late, but otherwise, SUCCESS! When he woke up, he got all the pomp and circumstance of a king! Well not quite, but needless to say, he felt very proud of himself and so were his Mom and Dad! Bedtime went equally well and before you knew it, it was just like tucking him in his crib. He stayed in bed all night, not getting books, opening cupboards or moving furniture, just sleeping. My little big boy!
